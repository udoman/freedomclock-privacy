Privacy Policy

FreedomClock App • Last updated: December 26, 2024

Your Privacy, Guaranteed

FreedomClock is 100% offline and collects zero data. Your financial information never leaves your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no tracking. Period.

Information We Collect

None.

FreedomClock does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information whatsoever. All data you enter into the app (including your financial figures, expense categories, and preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure on-device storage.

Data Storage

All information is stored locally on your iPhone using:

  • SwiftData - Apple's secure, on-device database
  • UserDefaults - For app preferences and widget data

Your data is protected by your device's passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID, and is included in your encrypted iPhone backups.

No Network Access

FreedomClock does not:

  • Connect to the internet
  • Send data to any servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Require an account or login
  • Access your contacts, photos, or location

Widget Data

If you use the FreedomClock widget, your financial summary is shared between the main app and the widget using Apple's App Groups feature. This data stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply delete the FreedomClock app from your device. All stored information will be permanently removed. There is no cloud backup to delete because none exists.

Third-Party Services

FreedomClock uses zero third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

Children's Privacy

FreedomClock does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no age-restricted content.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Given our commitment to zero data collection, significant changes are unlikely.
